Features

Autonomous by default.
Configurable by design.

Majordomo doesn't draft — it sends. Every feature is built around the assumption that your time is worth more than inbox triage.

Fully autonomous replies

No approval queue. No drafts sitting unreviewed. Majordomo reads incoming mail and sends a reply on your behalf — immediately.

Configurable persona & tone

Define your agent's voice once. Formal, warm, brief, detailed — each mailbox gets its own personality. The reply sounds like you, not a bot.

Smart escalation

When the agent encounters something outside its remit — a legal threat, an angry VIP, a genuinely novel situation — it escalates to you. Everything else, it handles.

EU-hosted & GDPR-compliant

Data stays in Europe. Processing agreements available. Right-to-erasure supported. Emails are processed in memory and never stored beyond what's needed.

Multi-mailbox support

Connect as many inboxes as you need. One deployment, dozens of mailboxes — each with its own rules, persona, and escalation policy.

Self-hostable

Stricter data requirements? Run Majordomo on your own infrastructure. Full stack, your servers, your control. No vendor lock-in.
